Tortilla Omelette Recipe

Are you a student staying in a student dormitory and now you have to cook for yourself or just someone exploring new recipes? You won't need a lot of ingredients or "heavy artillery" so let's get to cooking.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil - 1 spoon
  • Sausage - 50-100 grams
  • Eggs - 3 pcs
  • Cheese - 2-3 slices
  • Tortilla - 1 wrap
  • Chilli sauce - 1-2 spoons

Instructions

  1. Get everything ready. Cut the sausage into small pieces, Slice the cheese and stir the eggs for 1-2 minutes.
  2. Heat up the pan, put one spoon of olive oil and the cut sausage into the pan.
  3. Fry the sausage for 2-3 minutes, afterwards pour the stirred eggs all over the pan.
  4. Wait until the omelette is almost ready (5-10 minutes) and put the tortilla wrap above the omelette.
  5. Wait for 20-30 seconds and flip the tortilla with the omelette (by now they must be stuck together so you should be able to do that).
  6. Put the sliced cheese on the tortilla omelette and fold it.
  7. Fry for 30 seconds more then flip the tortilla omelette again. You can turn off the stove now.
  8. Fry for more 30 seconds then take it out of the pan, unfold the tortilla omelette, spread the chilli sauce all over it and fold it back.
  9. VOILA! Your meal is ready to be served.
